Sign To Speak

Bringing everyday tasks to the ones who can't.

A wearable arm sleeve prototype that converts sign language gestures into realtime audio conversation!

  • More about the project.

Benefits

Those who use sign language to communicate can break the language barrier and make progress toward a life without complications in such a crucial component: speech. The program allows for users to not cut out the sign language process but rather preserve it and present a solution to communicate to all with it.

Future

Transfer of the gesture-to-language program to a on-the-go app can make this a mobile and easy solution for communication. Pair that with a slimmer glove sleeve with integrated sensors can make life that much smoother for users.

Hardware

Included in the system is the myo armband to push emerging technology to new levels, bringing aid to people that would otherwise lack such a trivial thing without it.
